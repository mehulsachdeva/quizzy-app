Quizzy

Welcome to Quizzy ! Quizzy is an online learning tool for students where they can test their knowledge by taking quizes. They can check or analyse their knowledge i.e whether their knowledge is improving or not. It is the best platform to test their skills within stipulated time. One can see the statistics of their performances after taking quizes. It also helps the instructors or teachers to understand and visualize the transition of group of students during learning-teaching activity. Teachers can take regular quizes for overall development of student in specific area or field. Quizzy can help to understand the dynamics of student and their activities from the result of the regular tests.
